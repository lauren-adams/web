The Poppy War (The Poppy War, #1)

Author: R.F. Kuang

My Rating: 4

Date Read: 2023-06-08 00:00:00

My Review: I was not expecting to enjoy this book very much, I just picked it up because I recognized the cover. It is a fantasy retelling of China's history around WW2 covering the opium wars and the Sion-Chinese wars during WW2, with a little bit of magic and some really cool world building thrown in. There were parts of it that were just graphically and horribly violent, and made me think why am reading this, like this is so intense. But afterwards I learned that the violent atrocities were taken direct from accounts of the Rape of Nanjing and some other historical events in the Sino-Chinese wars. This is not a fun read or a light read, but it is a really great read and it really makes you think about a part of history we normally try not to think about. The characters make some stupid decisions, so that gets on my nerves sometimes, but they are enduring horrible war conditions so I guess they can make bad decisions. Anyways, it was dark, but I couldn't put it down and it's a really beautiful way at looking at the dark truths of war. 8.3
